# Impact of Exchange Rate and Inflation on Exports of India

## Overview

This econometrics project examines how exchange rate movements and inflation influence India's export performance.

The study uses annual Indian macroeconomic data from 1985–2021 and estimates multiple regression models to evaluate the relationship between exports, inflation, and exchange rates.

---

## Research Question

How do exchange rates and inflation affect India's exports?

---

## Data

Period: 1985–2021

Variables:
- Exports (%)
- Inflation (%)
- Exchange Rate (INR/USD)

---

## Methodology

### Econometric Techniques

- Ordinary Least Squares (OLS)
- Polynomial Regression Model
- ANOVA Analysis
- Confidence Intervals

### Diagnostic Tests

- White Test for Heteroskedasticity
- Breusch-Godfrey Test for Autocorrelation
- RESET Specification Test
- Multicollinearity Analysis

---

## Key Findings

- Exchange rate has a positive and statistically significant impact on exports.
- Inflation also shows a positive relationship with exports.
- Polynomial regression performs better than the linear model based on R² comparison.
- Exchange rate effects are stronger and more statistically significant than inflation effects.
